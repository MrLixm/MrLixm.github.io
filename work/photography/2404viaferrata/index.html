‚àß While we are on a quite flat portion of the trail, I'm taking the opportunity to turn back and take a shot at the distance we have covered. We are at the middle of the two massive cliffs surounding us and luckly, a shadowy area that is most welcomed after the previous part that was under a hard-hitting sun. I can even see the rope-bridge we took to join the other cliff side and that cross over the river that lines the bottom of that rift we are in.
