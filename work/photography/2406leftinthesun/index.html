Left in the Sun

spots of interesting concrete, blazed by our star

  • 2024-06

  • France - Lyon - Croix-Rousse

  • Kodak Portra 400 35mm

  • Minolta MD 35mm

  • Minolta X-500

‚àß I looked up and slighty above me was this opened window. Emerging from the shadow of the interior I noticed some tropical plants that probably occupy the most luminous place of the apartment. This led me to press the shutter. Now that I can view the result I really like how the film stock rendered the blue of the sky reflecting on the window, which go in pair with the typical french blue street sign attached on the left.

‚à® Ivy is an incredible plant. Eating up as much space as possible while defying gravity. This clump was interesting too shoot as being a very dense clump on a small area of a big white house, with a strong sun beam right on it ! Some stone walls in the foreground nicely layer the picture and give some charm to the composition.
