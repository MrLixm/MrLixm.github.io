The Offering (2023)

23 June 2023

My first movie credit ! A classical horror movie with a sick humanoid goat monster. I was working as a Lighting TD at WWFX UK for this project and was responsible for lighting on most of the shots with the creature. A bit sad that the screenshots clip are so compressed, but you know dark films and video codec compression ...
